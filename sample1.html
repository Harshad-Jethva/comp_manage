The CivicResolve Blueprint

Our streamlined, AI-driven process ensures your concerns are not just heard, but acted upon swiftly and transparently.

1. Submit with Ease

Use our intuitive form or AI Chatbot to report an issue. Upload photos, videos, and pinpoint the exact location on a map. Our system helps you provide all the necessary details for a quick resolution.

2. AI-Powered Analysis

Once submitted, our "Jarvis" AI engine analyzes the complaint's content, urgency, and location. It intelligently categorizes the issue and routes it to the pre-assigned official in the correct department, eliminating bureaucratic delays.

3. Transparent Tracking

Monitor the progress of your complaint in real-time on your personal dashboard. Receive automated notifications at every stage, from "In Review" to "Work in Progress" and finally "Resolved". Your feedback is requested upon completion.
